Our Electrical Drafting Support and Services

For over 20 years, Flatworld Solutions has been acclaimed as the leading electrical drafting service providing company available in India as well as almost all countries worldwide. Mentioned below are some of the support and services that we can be contacted for -

Electrical Schematics

As per regulations, it is mandatory to divulge all electrical schematics before any installation or wiring work can be proceeded with. We provide a detailed overview of electrical power and lighting plans based on the construction. This will give you the best possible powering/ lighting-up options be it for homes, companies, organizations, or shop displays, etc. We make use of the latest technology to give you schematic representations of electrical equipment in a building covering the displays, floors, elevators, escalators, solar panels, and wall section, as well as the equipment to be attached.

AutoCAD Electrical Drafting

Our engineers accurately design schematic diagrams of electrical plans, wirings drawings, and lighting set-up diagrams using the latest CAD software. Employing 3D modeling and animation you can view the structure and its electrical outlets before any installations.

Electronic Prototyping Services

We create prototypes and use them to test the functionality and operation. This will enable you to understand if the electrical supply is optimum for your equipment and whether all electrical specifications are met as per the set criteria.

Control Panel Design

This is important in understanding the distribution of power and energy efficiency. We develop customized designs as per your requirement and/ or the one we consider as the best option. Our engineers are adept at working with different panel types among which are PLC, VFD, distribution board, AC drive, and wiring drawing machine control, etc.

Electrical Wiring Diagrams

All electrical drafting designs comply with rules and regulations of the location in which the work is undertaken. We stringently follow engineering/ architectural standards and BOMA/ IFMA standards.

Maintenance and Repairs

We undertake electrical drafts for already existing buildings/ structures that require rewiring and/ or alternate ways of lighting. In case of any additional equipment that needs to be included, we help you take the best alternative.

Earthing & Lighting Protection

All earthing system layouts, pits interconnection drawings, layouts of earthing strips, and lighting protection structural drafts are provided. This ensures the electrical aspects are safe and hazard-free.

Fire Protection System Layout

We give you detailed schematic representations of where all fire protectors and sprinklers can be installed. Likewise, the best visual representation where the smoke detectors and alarms can be incorporated.

Communication Systems

We provide drafts of all audio and visual system designs for intercom, CCTV, and public connectivity. All representations provided by our team will ensure that there is no damage due to flawless electrical wiring.

Electrical Data Archiving

Our team is here to help you prioritize all the work, keep track of documentation, and also help store it for future reference.

Our Electrical Drafting Process

The process followed by us for providing our electrical drafting services is as mentioned below -

1. Understanding Client Requirement

We consult with you/ your team and understand the magnitude of the work that needs to be undertaken to draft an electrical plan that is efficient and conserves energy as well.

2. Analysis

We thoroughly study the structure that needs to be powered up to give you the best conservational electrical plan.

3. Development and Design

We develop the most appropriate electrical drafts. We create drafts as per specifications given by you and provide inputs on improvisation, usage of raw materials, and choice of equipment.

4. 3D Visuals

Our presentation of the 2D as well as a 3D format of the structure will give you a glimpse into future construction.

5. Quality Checks

Any changes/ revisions are done as desired by the client after drafting a precise layout of all electrical work.

6. Final Draft

The final draft is sent across securely to complete the project cycle.
