Digitization Services

Preserve Your Drawings

Our paper to CAD conversion services enable you to create permanent digital records. We protect your drawings from damage and sabotage, reduce the need for physical storage, and minimize data loss by 99%. VMTL uses technical proficiency and best-in-class scanning equipment to ensure clear and accurate outcomes.

Our Capabilities

Services We Offer

Scanning Paper Drawings

We use best-in-class scanning equipment to handle drawings that are too small, faint, or illegible, ensuring clear and accurate digital outcomes.

Pre-conversion Clean-up

Before converting hand-drawn plans, we remove speckles, fill in missing gaps in lines, and fix basic errors to ensure impeccable results.

DFX/DWG Conversion

We convert paper drawings into numerous vector formats including DFX and DWG, following carefully laid-out steps for seamless automation.

PDF to CAD Conversion

Transform PDF files into fully editable CAD formats, ensuring precision in design modifications and optimizing your workflow.

Image to CAD Conversion

Convert raster images (JPEG, PNG, TIFF) into vector CAD files, preserving the accuracy and detail of your original designs.

2D to 3D Conversion

Upgrade your legacy 2D drawings into intelligent 3D models for better visualization, simulation, and manufacturing.
