Accurate 3D Models

VMTL is one of the most preferred outsourcing firms in the field of engineering, offering customized 3D part modeling services that ensure 100% accuracy. Our expertise guarantees improved design quality, ensuring considerable cost savings. We provide solutions for Automobile, Manufacturing, Consumer Goods, and Aerospace industries.

Our Capabilities

Services We Offer

Multi-view Representation

Multi-view representation of machine parts (balloons and leader lines) in 3D to ensure comprehensive visualization and documentation.

3D Machine Parts Modeling

3D machine parts modeling for automotive components and other industrial fixtures, customized to meet specific manufacturing requirements.

Assembly Modeling

3D modeling for assemblies and collection of parts, ensuring accurate fit, function, and interference checks before production.

Industry-Specific Modeling

3D modeling for components in Aerospace, Automobile, Electronics, etc., tailored to the unique standards of each sector.

Sheet Metal & Plastic

3D modeling for sheet metal and plastic molding, optimizing designs for manufacturability and material efficiency.

2D to 3D Modeling

3D modeling from 2D details, converting legacy drawings and sketches into accurate, editable 3D CAD models.

Sectional Views

3D CAD models and sectional views to reveal internal details and complex geometries for better analysis.
