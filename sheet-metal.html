Sheet Metal Design Services We Offer

At VMTL, we have always delivered high-quality robust designs that are compliant with international standards. We have provided quality services to businesses across various industry sectors such as automobile, aerospace, electronics, power, transport, and telecom. Some of the key sheet metal product design services we offer include -

Component Designing

Our team of mechanical engineers has the required skills, expertise, and resources to provide our clients with comprehensive product design services. Right from prototype development, metal selection, sheet cutting design, to helping you with designing parts and assemblies, we can help you at every stage.

CAD/CAM Solutions

Our team of engineers at VMTL can provide you with comprehensive and top-quality CAD/CAM solutions. We provide CAD and 3D modeling services using all the leading software in the industry. In addition, we also provide product implementation training and guidance to clients and help them establish a robust design and development framework.

Design Technical Support

We have a dedicated team which will provide you with the required technical support regarding your metal sheet design needs. Our resources are also capable of providing you with project re-engineering and value engineering support. We can also help you with all the 2D and 3D file import, upload, and download of the engineering drawings on to the FTP server.

Our Systematic Approach for Sheet Metal Design

We believe in providing our clients with the best and top-quality services within a quick turnaround time. Doing so, we help our clients to save a considerable amount of time and money which would have been invested in hiring an in-house team of engineers and designers. We follow a systematic and streamlined process while providing sheet metal product design services to our clients, which include the following steps -

1
Identify design parameters and specifications
2
Develop different design solutions
3
Evaluate the developed design solutions
4
Design for manufacturability (DFM)
5
Optimize the product design and manufacturing processes
6
Prepare cost estimates based on material, number of operations, etc.
7
Calculate the adequate size of corner reliefs, bend angles, and radii
8
Create 3D sheet metal model and complete 2D flat drawings
