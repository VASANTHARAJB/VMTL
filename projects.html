A TRACK RECORD OF DELIVERING RELIABLE ENGINEERING

ISO Vibration Analysis

Blower Plant Vibration

Study of external and internal vibrations for a plant with multiple blowers. We performed Natural frequency analysis (Modal) and Dynamic response analysis (Harmonic) using ANSYS to identify critical locations and suggest ISO-standard corrections.

Reverse Engineering

Aerospace Components

Recreating obsolete aircraft spare parts. Using physical scanning, we converted 2D data to high-precision 3D CAD models, enabling the manufacturing of improved spare parts with a reduced lead time of one month.

Product Design

High-Altitude Backpack

Designed a customized heat-resistant backpack for high-altitude use within a rapid 2-week lead time using SolidWorks. The design met all requirements and secured the client's contract for production.

Design Optimization

Centrifugal Pump Design

Redesigned an Indian Railways pump for higher efficiency. Through 15 CFD simulation iterations on impeller vanes and housing, we achieved 125% output without changing motor power consumption.

Digital Factory

Plant Level CAD Design

Converted an existing large manufacturing plant into a 3D model and digital asset. This 3D visualization helped the client improve plant processes, creating significant cost savings and enabling 3D animation.

FEA & CFD Analysis

Thermoforming Simulation

Performed advanced PolyFlow analysis using ANSYS to improve plastic sheet thickness in thermoforming. This helped the manufacturer get the design right the first time, leading to repeat business.
