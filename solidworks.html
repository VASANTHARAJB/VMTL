SolidWorks Expertise

VMTL provides comprehensive SolidWorks design services, ensuring precision, speed, and scalability in CAD design. Our Capabilities

Services We Offer

3D SolidWorks Modeling

Transformation of initial ideas from sketches into accurate, production-ready 3D models. We can also help create 3D models from a master design model using Excel design tables.

3D Assembly Services

Assemblies ranging from a few parts to thousands of components to produce accurate 3D assemblies. This enables clash detection, center-of-gravity analysis, and early-stage structural validation.

2D Drawings & Blueprints

Exceptionally accurate 2D drawings and blueprints back-linked and updated from a 3D CAD model. Includes bespoke title blocks, smart-property printing, and complete legacy component integration.

Advanced 3D Surfacing

Selectively manipulate 3D surfaces to produce simple or complex parts. Ideal for plastic injection molding and sculpted features in machine tools or consumer electronics using Class-A surfacing.

Sheet Metal Part Services

Precision-driven, intricate 3D designs for sheet metal parts, converted into flat 2D prints with bend lines for seamless brake press operations and optional files for plasma/laser cutting.

Weldment Part Services

Accurate 3D weldments with detailed 2D blueprints to aid cutting, assembly, and welding on the floor. Designs range from compact frames to multi-story enclosures.

3D Printing Design Services

Photorealistic Design
