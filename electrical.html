Electrical Engineering Design Services We Offer

Explore our end-to-end electrical engineering services, designed to handle complex electrical projects with uncompromised accuracy and safety. Optimized for energy efficiency and smooth smart technology integration, our comprehensive electrical engineering solutions include:

Electrical Design and Layout Services

Meet precision and safety standards in your projects with professionally created layouts for low, medium, and high voltage systems. With state-of-the-art CAD software and engineering practices, we provide optimized designs that minimize errors, lower risks, and ensure durability.

Electrical Drafting & Schematic Services

Error-free drafting solutions leveraging advanced electrical engineering tools. We deliver accurate power and lighting plans, load estimations, and fixture layouts.

Electrical 2D Drafting Services

Streamline your project workflows with precise 2D plans for small circuits, power distribution, and layouts. Using leading tools like ProDesign, AutoCAD, and SmartDraw, our experts offer accurate designs for better coordination and easy execution of electrical works with any degree of complexity.

PCB Design and Layout Services

Enhance product performance with customized PCB layouts that are optimized for reliability and efficiency. From schematic capture to prototype fabrication and certified engineering skill, we guarantee to meet functional specifications, decrease iterations, and get your product to market faster.

PCB Reverse Engineering Services

Salvage valuable design information and enhance current systems through cutting-edge PCB reverse engineering. We are updated with the leading electrical engineering software and equipment to assist in modernizing boards, solving supply chain issues, and designing cost-saving alternatives for sustained production.

Power Engineering Services

Robust and reliable power infrastructure with solutions encompassing generation, transmission, and distribution. Our experts work on high-end simulation tools and lean-optimized design techniques to maximize performance and minimize energy losses.

Power Distribution Services

Carefully designed power distribution plans customized to your needs and optimized for efficiency. Through load demand analysis and optimized routing, we guarantee balanced distribution, minimized downtime, and scalable infrastructure.

Electrical Grounding System Design Services

Secure your assets and personnel through grounding systems that leverage the latest testing and design methods. We design safe grounding solutions to help firms maintain compliance with safety standards and prevent losses caused by electrical failures.

Solar Panel Design Services

Optimize renewable energy yield with tailored solar panel configurations based on residential, commercial, and industrial applications. Our designs integrate site conditions, load demands, and cutting-edge modeling to provide efficient and scalable solar power solutions.

Lighting Control and Design Services

Smart energy management with advanced lighting control design tailored to the needs of various spaces, like commercial and industrial environments. Our lighting control includes dimming systems, occupancy sensors, and IoT-enabled lighting modules for responsive and energy-efficient solutions.

Smart Panel Layout Drawing Service

Gain better energy management with custom smart panel layouts designed for IoT integration. Using advanced electrical engineering software, we deliver intuitive and flexible panel schematics that support monitoring, control, and optimization across diverse applications.

Electrical Calibration Services

Rest assured of the precision and compliance of your equipment with our professional calibration services. Our technical experts, possessing state-of-the-art testing tools, deliver fast, reliable calibration to maximize your equipment life and reduce downtime.

Electrical Equipment Support Services

Tailored support for electrical equipment installation, commissioning, and lifecycle maintenance. Our electrical engineers leverage the best tools with the industry's best practices to perform calibration, fault diagnosis, and compliance testing, driving uptime and increasing your asset life.

Control System Engineering & Architecture Services

Advanced control system architecture tailored with the latest electrical engineering tools for industrial automation. We design and integrate Programmable Logic Controllers (PLCs), Supervisory Control and Data Acquisition (SCADA) systems, and Distributed Control Systems (DCS) for seamless coordination and minimal downtime.

Electrical Instrumentation Services

Precision-driven instrumentation services for designing, installing, calibrating, and maintaining instruments and devices for accurate measurements and monitoring of electrical and industrial processes. We conduct rigorous quality checks to ensure functional reliability to help our clients meet demanding project specifications.

Field Instrument Location Services

Enhance project success through appropriately mapped instrument locations, plans, and charts. Using systematic analysis and sophisticated drafting, we provide reliable layouts that promote easy installation, minimize the risk of misplacement, and facilitate smooth project implementation.

Energy Modeling Services

Innovative modeling technologies optimized to help enterprises maintain high standards of sustainability. Utilizing best-in-class tools and industry standards such as ASHRAE and LEED, our simulations support green building certifications and comply with changing standards of energy management.

Our Electrical Engineering Design Process

We work with a systematic, client-centric methodology that delivers safe, efficient, and cost-effective electrical engineering services. Every phase of our project lifecycle is properly planned to minimize risks, speed up project timelines, and ensure maximum performance. Here is how we do it:

1
Requirement Analysis

We thoroughly analyze your project requirements, client expectations, and compliance requirements to identify potential issues up front. This allows us to create a plan to meet your timelines, budgets, and industry standards.

2
Concept Development

We take your requirements and develop effective system concepts, incorporating critical constraints such as load balancing, sustainability goals, and compatibility with a building or factory plan.

3
Detailed Design

Our experts use their relevant field knowledge along with AutoCAD technology and electrical CAD to create accurate layouts, wiring diagrams, and circuit designs. Our designs are accurate, scalable, and optimized for safety, performance, and longevity.

4
Implementation Planning

We work with contractors, developers, and designers to develop application-ready execution plans, including equipment selection, compliance checks, and measures to mitigate risk, consequently decreasing installation impacts and maximizing operational efficiencies.

5
System Delivery

Our specialists manage electrical systems installation and integration without compromising on quality, compliance, or the handover process.

6
Ongoing Support

After delivering our electrical engineering services, we offer post-service support like preventive maintenance, troubleshooting, and upgrade support to extend your equipment life cycles.
