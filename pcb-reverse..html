PCB Reverse Engineering Services We Offer

VMTL is a global leader for multi-purpose PCB cloning, inspection, and measurement systems. Outsource your requirement to us. Our services include:

Disassembling Components

Our experts make a detailed record of components - their location number, package, temperature, and value. They scan and save a backup of the existing PCB, after which they start disassembling the components.

Preparing BOM

The next step in the reverse engineering mechanism is to prepare a BOM. Our experts take your sample PCB and reverse engineer it down to the last component. Then, we assemble a bill of materials for the project that includes the measured values for resistors and capacitors.

Shade Analysis

Take the assistance of our experts to design an optimized solar panel layout that minimizes shading issues, maximizes energy production, and ensures the overall effectiveness of your system.

Physical Circuit Board (PCB) Duplication

Not just the BOM, our experts reversely analyze the circuit boards and look into other engineering and production documents like the original PCB file, schematic file, PCB Gerber, and PCB silkscreen.

Using A Reverse Engineering Software

Further, we prep and scan the surface of the PCB board and put the images through copying software. We adopt the perfect inspection method to produce high-quality PCB drawings. We leverage PCB drawing software, image processing software, and the physical connection of the circuit to make an accurate judgment.

PCB Testing Services

We test the PCB and scrutinize the dielectric of the substrate constant to ensure that the newly engineered PCB indicators are consistent with the original board.
