Innovation & Ideation

Transforming Ideas into Reality

Concept design is the crucial initial phase of product development where ideas take shape. At VMTL, we specialize in transforming abstract concepts into tangible, viable product designs. Our team combines creativity with engineering precision to deliver innovative solutions that meet market demands and manufacturing feasibility.

Our Capabilities

Services We Offer

Design for Control Panel

We design human interfaces like touch-screens, keypads, and knob controllers essential for plant machinery as per specifications.

Concept Design

We prototype abstract ideas using 3D CAD modeling into functional models that allow clients to visualize their idea in action.

Phase Out Management

We ensure controlled phase-out via engineering change management, legacy drawing conversion, BOM management, and RoHS services.

Product Design for Plastic

Designing plastic assemblies and housing from conceptualization to molding and fabrication with exact specifications.

Thermal & Structural Analysis

Expert CAE structural analysis to ensure designs comply with environmental regulations and estimate product life cycle.

Industrial Design Solution

Leveraging expertise for mass production designs that improve reliability while minimizing operational and production costs.

Sheet Metal Housing

Cost-friendly assembly designs for electronic components, making enclosures lightweight and safe for sensitive circuits.

Evaluation of Reliability

DFMEA and FMEA tests to understand tolerances, improve device longevity, and troubleshoot hidden design flaws.

Concept Design for Chassis

Designing chassis and frames of exact weight and strength to lower the cost of fabrication and mass production.
