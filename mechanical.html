Our Comprehensive Mechanical Engineering Design Services

Flatworld Solutions has been one of the leading computational fluid dynamics service providers to clients around the globe for over 20 years. We understand each client's unique requirements and provide them with services that will suit their business requirements. Some of the key computational fluid dynamics solutions we offer include -

CAD Conversion Services

Reduce overhead and accelerate design cycles by converting paper-based or legacy formats into precise CAD files. Minimal error and maximum efficiency.

2D Drafting Services

Highly detailed technical drawings including architectural and preliminary plans. Flexible for design iterations and compliance documentation.

3D CAD Modeling

Detailed 3D representations to visualize parts, test tolerances, check interference, and improve cross-department communication.

3D Rendering

Photorealistic visuals to help architects, builders, and marketers enable faster designs and stakeholder approvals.

CAD Design Services

End-to-end CAD design services such as CAE integration, concept generation, fixture and mold design, and prototyping.

Reverse Engineering

Precise reconstruction of digital models from physical components using 3D scanning, benchmarking, and parametric modeling.

Design for Manufacturing

Refined designs for manufacturability with part standardization and material optimization to ensure efficient production.

Rapid Prototyping

Accelerate product validation with quick-turn prototypes and simulation-based testing to verify performance before scaling.

The Process We Follow to Deliver Mechanical Engineering Services

At Flatworld, we follow a standardized workflow proven for streamlining operations and cutting costs while delivering high-quality mechanical engineering services. Here is our process flow that ensures our support is aligned with your project goals, technical requirements, and compliance standards:

01
Requirement Analysis

We start with understanding and outlining your business objectives, technical specifications, and project constraints. This helps us customize our mechanical engineering services based on your compliance needs, potential engineering challenges, and long-term performance goals.

02
Strategic Planning

Our engineers and project leads prepare a road map to structure our execution, choosing the right tools, experts, and delivery models. This helps us optimize our mechanical engineering services for speed, accuracy, and resource efficiency.

03
Design & Development

Strategic plans are translated into action using CAD modeling, simulation, and virtual prototyping. We engineer every component of design and development for performance and scalability.

04
Implementation

After defining the clear SLAs, our engineers execute detailed tasks across modeling, drafting, analysis, and documentation. We ensure transparency and alignment with your goals at every phase of the execution process for your stakeholders.

05
Quality Assurance

Being an ISO-certified company, every deliverable is rigorously tested and reviewed using automated and manual checks by experts. This helps us check and verify design accuracy, simulation results, and GD&T standards.

06
Delivery & Support

Final outputs are delivered in the preferred format securely through encrypted cloud or FTP channels. Post delivery, we also provide support such as updates, format changes, or documentation requests.
