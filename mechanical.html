Our Comprehensive Mechanical Engineering Design Services

VMTL has been one of the leading computational fluid dynamics service providers to clients around the globe for over 20 years. We understand each client's unique requirements and provide them with services that will suit their business requirements.

CAD Conversion Services

Reduce overhead and accelerate design cycles by converting paper-based or legacy formats into precise CAD files.

2D Drafting Services

Highly detailed technical drawings including architectural and preliminary plans. Flexible for design iterations.

3D CAD Modeling

Detailed 3D representations to visualize parts, test tolerances, check interference, and improve communication.

3D Rendering

Photorealistic visuals to help architects, builders, and marketers enable faster designs and stakeholder approvals.

CAD Design Services

End-to-end CAD design services such as CAE integration, concept generation, fixture and mold design.

Reverse Engineering

Precise reconstruction of digital models from physical components using 3D scanning and parametric modeling.

Design for Manufacturing

Refined designs for manufacturability with part standardization and material optimization to ensure efficient production.

Rapid Prototyping

Accelerate product validation with quick-turn prototypes and simulation-based testing to verify performance.

The Process We Follow

At Flatworld, we follow a standardized workflow proven for streamlining operations and cutting costs while delivering high-quality mechanical engineering services.

01
Requirement Analysis

We start with understanding your business objectives, technical specifications, and project constraints.

02
Strategic Planning

Our engineers prepare a roadmap to structure execution, choosing the right tools and delivery models.

03
Design & Development

Strategic plans are translated into action using CAD modeling, simulation, and virtual prototyping.

04
Implementation

Execution of detailed tasks across modeling, drafting, analysis, and documentation.

05
Quality Assurance

Rigorously tested and reviewed using automated and manual checks by experts.

06
Delivery & Support

Final outputs delivered securely, followed by post-delivery support and updates.
