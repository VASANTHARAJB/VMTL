Reverse Engineering Solutions We Offer

Our reverse engineering services address a wide range of engineering needs‚Äîfrom competitive benchmarking and design optimization to compliance validation and DFMA-based cost reduction‚Äîhelping you turn physical parts into intelligent digital assets.

3D Scanning Services

We use portable CMMs, 3D laser scanners, and CT systems to capture high-accuracy internal and external geometries of legacy, worn, or complex parts. This data forms the basis for CAD reconstruction, validation, or digital twin generation.

CAD Modeling Services

Validated scan data is converted into intelligent 2D and 3D CAD models using parametric or hybrid techniques based on project needs. We deliver models in IGES, STEP, SolidWorks, or native formats for easy downstream use.

Dimensional Analysis Services

We apply GD&T-based analysis to detect geometric deviations, validate tolerances, and confirm part accuracy against design requirements. This ensures each reverse-engineered model meets form, fit, and functional benchmarks.

Computer-Aided Inspection (CAI & CAV)

3D scan outputs are compared to nominal CAD using deviation mapping, overlays, and cross-sections to ensure dimensional fidelity. Critical elements like threads and interfaces are validated for material compatibility.

Software Reverse Engineering

Decompile firmware or dissect system protocols, networks, and control logic. Ideal for legacy aerospace or defense systems where source code or documentation is unavailable.

Drone Component & UAV Reverse Engineering

Recreate 3D models and subsystems of drone airframes, motors, or sensors for proprietary UAV platforms. Accelerates OEM refurbishment, compliance validation, and performance improvements.

Detailed Dimensional & Geometric Analysis

High-precision dimensional data and GD&T insights for critical components. This service supports tolerance validation, deformation detection, and fit verification‚Äîvital for aerospace and automotive.

Material and Surface Analysis

Examine surface properties like material, coatings, and finishes through destructive and non-destructive methods. Helps with replicating a part that operates under any performance requirement or compliance constraint.

Design & Manufacturing Optimization (DFMA)

Combines part geometry and tolerance checks with Design for Manufacturing & Assembly (DFMA) analysis to enhance producibility across CNC, molding, or additive methods.

Prototyping

Conversion of verified CAD models into functional physical prototypes using 3D printing, machining, or hybrid processes. We help engineers bridge the gap between digital design and production validation.

In addition to standard reverse engineering solutions, we also offer:

Legacy Digitization & Digital Twin

Transforms legacy aerospace, defense, and heavy equipment components into high-fidelity digital twins for simulation, predictive maintenance, and lifecycle extension.

Failure & Lifecycle Analysis

Identifies failure modes and estimates component lifespan to support proactive redesign and root cause analysis in critical systems like automotive and aerospace.

Electronics / PCB Reverse Engineering

Extracts board layouts, circuit logic, and embedded design features from existing PCBs, meeting demands in electronics, medtech, and semiconductor applications.

Our 3D Reverse Engineering Services Techniques

Our advanced scanning and mechanical reverse engineering services are designed to meet the precision, speed, and compliance needs of modern engineering and manufacturing. Here are our key techniques to deliver exceptional accuracy and efficiency:

3D Laser Scanning

Capture complex geometries and free-form surfaces with high-resolution datasets. For non-contact digitization of intricate parts or fragile surfaces.

CMM Scanning

Get ultra-precise dimensional data using programmable probes. Especially ideal for high-precision driven applications in aerospace/automotive.

Portable CMM Scanning

Perform measurement and inspection on-site for objects that are difficult to move. Ideal for plant-level assessments and rapid turnaround projects.

Full Contact CMM Scanning

Provides tactile measurements of great repeatability for prismatic pieces or rigid parts where surface precision is key to check machined features.

Non-Contact Inspection

Fast and non-invasive scanning to protect delicate and soft-surface parts. We ensure quality control in inspection without posing risk of damage.

Electronic & Electrical Analysis

Investigation of circuits, identification of components, and schematic reconstruction. Restore legacy PCBs with missing documentation.

Our Lean-Based Reverse Engineering Process

We follow a lean-optimized, quality-focused process that eliminates inefficiencies and delivers reliable digital models ready for any application. Here is the 6-step process we follow:

1
Receive Parts

We log components and review them for criticality and material sensitivity, ensuring a clear technical context.

2
Scanning

Using high-precision 3D laser scanners or CMMs, we extract and document accurate dimensional data.

3
Data Validation

Raw scan data is filtered and aligned to purge noise, setting a clean foundation for modeling.

4
Modeling & Analysis

We create parametric 2D/3D models from validated data for tests, inspection, or reproduction.

5
Quality Verification

Generated models are compared against scan data via CAI to validate material fit and conformance.

6
Delivery

Final CAD files (IGES, STEP, etc.) and reports are shared, ensuring seamless integration with your PLM systems.
