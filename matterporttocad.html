Our Matterport to CAD Conversion Services

Matterport to CAD conversion involves the use of different technologies and approaches. We believe that specialized real estate models require specialized approaches.

Matterport to 3DS Conversion

3DS is one of the older CAD formats and still manages to be popularly employed in modern real estate models. We can have your Matterport models exported and converted to 3DS within minutes.

Matterport to FBX Conversion

FBX is an important proprietary format used by Autodesk and accepted by CAD. We take pride in having a track record of numerous successful Matterport to CAD conversions catering to this format.

Black and White Floor Plan Preparation

Floor plans are a crucial component of CAD architectural drawing. Our team specializes in developing professional, precise, and compelling black and white floor plans from any Matterport space.

Interactive 3D Tours

Our virtual tour team specializes in developing 3D videos from Matterport models to further enhance the immersive property viewing experience for real estate marketing.

Our Matterport to CAD Conversion Process

We offer exclusively designed conversion plans. Our generic framework, however, involves the following steps:

According to the findings, we determine the Autodesk file format that's best for your designs and map out the conversion process.
