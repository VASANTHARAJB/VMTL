Accelerate Innovation

Prototyping & Rapid Prototyping

Accelerate product development with VMTL's prototyping services. From initial concept models to fully functional prototypes, we help you visualize, test, and refine your designs before mass production. Our rapid prototyping capabilities ensure quick turnaround times, reducing time-to-market and minimizing costly design errors.

Our Capabilities

Services We Offer

Rapid Prototyping Services

We provide high-quality rapid prototyping services using advanced 3D printing and additive manufacturing technologies. This allows for quick iteration and validation of design concepts, helping you bring products to market faster.

3D Printing / Additive Manufacturing

Our state-of-the-art 3D printing capabilities cover various materials and technologies (SLA, SLS, FDM) to produce accurate and durable prototypes suitable for functional testing and aesthetic review.

Functional Prototyping

We create fully functional prototypes that mimic the properties of the final product. These are essential for rigorous testing of mechanical performance, fit, and assembly before investing in expensive tooling.

Visual Models

High-fidelity visual models for marketing, presentations, and stakeholder approval. We focus on surface finish, color, and texture to deliver a realistic representation of the final product.

Workflow

Rapid Prototyping Process

01

Requirement Analysis

We analyze your CAD files and project requirements to determine the best prototyping method and materials.

02

Prototype Creation

Using selected technologies (3D printing, CNC, etc.), we fabricate the prototype with high precision.

03

Quality Check

Rigorous inspection of dimensions, finish, and functionality to ensure it meets specifications.

04

Final Delivery

Secure packaging and delivery of the prototype to your location for review and testing.
